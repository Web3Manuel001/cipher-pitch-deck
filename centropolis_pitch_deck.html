THE PROBLEM

Crypto traders are drowning in complexity:

  • Information Overload: Hundreds of new tokens launch daily with conflicting signals and complex on-chain data
  • Expertise Barrier: Tools like TradingView, Nansen, and Dune Analytics require significant technical knowledge
  • Fragmented Experience: Traders juggle multiple platforms for analysis, social sentiment, and execution
  • Costly Mistakes: Rug pulls and scams cost retail traders billions of dollars annually

OUR SOLUTION

Cipher combines three core elements into one unified interface:

1. AI Analysis Engine (The Sentinel)

Instant rug detection, technical analysis, wallet tracking, and safety scoring

2. One-Click Execution

Integrated DEX aggregation (Jupiter) for instant trades with MEV protection

3. Social Intelligence

Real-time trending tokens, query velocity tracking, and community sentiment analysis

Result: Institutional-grade analysis accessible through a simple, conversational interface
